Shrimp Tempura

Delicious Steamed Buns

Shrimp tempura is a delicious and crunchy seafood dish!

Ingredients you will need:

  • Shrimp
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
    •

    Steps to fry your tempura:

    1. Defrost shrimp if necessary.
    2. Mix your eggs, water, and flour into batter. Do not over mix.
    3. Coat shrimp in batter when finished.
    4. Pan fry or deep fry shrimp until golden.
    5. Finally, let cool and enjoy!
