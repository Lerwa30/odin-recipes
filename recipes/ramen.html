Ramen

Hot and Hearty Ramen

Ramen is a delicious and filling Japanese style soup that is sure to please!

Ingredients you will need:

  • BBQ Pork
  • Egg Noodles
  • 1 Egg
  • Chicken Broth
  • Seaweed and Green Onion
    •

    Steps to make your ramen:

    1. Pan fry sliced pork with bbq sauce or seasoning.
    2. Heat up chicken broth with option spices.
    3. Boil egg noodles in separate pot.
    4. Mince green onions.
    5. Hardboil 1-2 eggs.
    6. Onc ingredients are done add to hot broth.
    7. Finally, add seaweed or fishcake to garnish you ramen dish.
